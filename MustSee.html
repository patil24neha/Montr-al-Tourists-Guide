Walk on St-Paul Street


Old-Montreal has so many things to see and do, but if you find yourself a bit short on time, a walk along Saint-Paul Street will be a great option to cram as much of the famous European atmosphere of Montreal. There are tons of restaurants, bars, souvenir shops and art galleries along the way for your enjoyment, and since Montreal is a cheap city to visit and live in, prices are quite fair even for a super touristy area.

Visit Basilique Notre-Dame


The Notre-Dame Basilica is one of the finest of its kind, with one of the most ornate gothic facades in the world, and a strikingly beautiful chapel at the back. Also, that's where Celine Dion got married.

Montreal biodome - space for life


Thrill to the sounds of the Tropical Rainforest, admire the lynx, beavers and otters in the Laurentian Maple Forest, dive into the underwater world of the Gulf of St. Lawrence and say hello to the frolicking penguins.
